C = control
M = esc or alt key

Highlighting text: C-space 
Copying highlighted text: M-w
Pasting copied text: C-y
Cutting highlighted text: C- w


Turning on line numbers: M-x linum-mode 

undo: C-x u

syntax highlighting 
M-x c-mode
M-x cpp-mode


Exit command mode: C-g

Goto line: M-g g

Erase until end of line: C-k
